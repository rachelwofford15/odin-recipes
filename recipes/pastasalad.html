Pasta Salad

Ingredients

  • 12oz Bowtie Pasta
  • 1 Small Can Black Olives
  • Cherry Tomatoes
  • Green Olives
  • Mini Pepperoni
  • Zesty Italian Dressing Mix
  • Oil
  • Vinegar
  • Feta Cheese Crumbles

Directions

  1. Cook pasta per directions on box

  2. Slice: cherry tomatoes and green olives

  3. Add olives, tomatoes, pepperoni, and feta cheese in a mixing bowl

  4. Sprinkle one package of Italian seasoning on mixture

  5. Add cooked, drained, rinsed pasta

  6. Mix one package of Italian seasoning with oil and vinegar per directions on package

  7. Stir and serve
