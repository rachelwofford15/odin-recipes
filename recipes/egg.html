Breakfast Egg Ring

Ingredients

  • Two Packages of Crescent Rolls
  • 8-10 Eggs
  • 6-8 Sausage Patties
  • 1 Cup Shredded Cheese
  • 1 Egg White
  • Optional: Diced Cooked Veggies (Onions, Peppers, Mushroom, Spinach)

Directions

  1. Cook eggs and sausage; mix

  2. Unroll crescent rolls; separate into 16 triangles

  3. Arrange triangles, slightly overlapping in a circle on a large round stone with wide ends 4 inches from edge

  4. Roll wide ends of dough toward center leaving an open circle in the middle

  5. Scoop egg misture on ring

  6. Bring points of triangle up over filling and tuck point under dough at center

  7. Brush with egg whites

  8. Bake at 375 for 25-30 minuites
