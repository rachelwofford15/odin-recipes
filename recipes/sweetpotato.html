Sweet Potato Casserole

Ingredients

  • Casserole
    • 3 Cups Mashed Sweet Potatoes (Yams)
    • 1 Cup Sugar
    • 1/2 tsp Salt
    • 2 Eggs
    • 1/2 Stick Butter
    • 1 tsp Vanilla
    • 1/2 tsp Nutmeg
    • 1/2 tsp Cinnamon
  • Topping
    • 1 Cup Coconut
    • 1/2 Cup Self-Rising Flour
    • 1/2 Stick Butter
    • 1 Cup Nuts (chopped)

Directions

  1. Mix all casserole ingredients

  2. Mix topping ingredients

  3. Sprinkle topping on top

  4. Bake at 300 for 35 minutes
